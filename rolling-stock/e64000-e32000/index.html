Rolling Stock E64000 / E32000

E64000 and E32000 are 10 and 5 cars versions of the EMU's built for the Marmaray project. The contract was signed in 2008 and the deliveries were made in 2011.

Type EMU
Amount of cars E32000 - 5 cars, E64000 - 10 cars
Max. Speed 95 km/h
Operation Speed 80 km/h
Weight E32000 - 198.68t, E64000 - 386.11t
Train Protection Systems CBTC (used with ATO), ERTMS L1 (not used in normal operations)
